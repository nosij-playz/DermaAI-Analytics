About the AI Model

Our deep learning model uses convolutional neural networks trained on thousands of labeled dermoscopic images from the ISIC archive. The system can classify multiple types of skin lesions with high accuracy and provides confidence scores for each prediction.

About Our Technology

Two-Stage Detection Process

Our system employs a sophisticated two-stage analysis:

  • Stage 1: Binary classification (Cancer vs. Non-Cancer)
  • Stage 2: Multi-class classification (Specific lesion type)

This approach ensures both high sensitivity for detection and specificity for classification.
